# 🚀 30 Days of AI Mastery

A complete and structured 30-day roadmap to master Artificial Intelligence, LLMs, and AI Engineering concepts.

---

## 📌 What You Will Learn

- AI, ML, Deep Learning Fundamentals
- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Transformers Architecture
- Embeddings & Vector Databases
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Prompt Engineering Techniques
- AI Agents & Memory Systems
- LangChain & MCP
- n8n AI Automation Workflows
- Fine-tuning & Model Optimization
- Model Deployment & LLMOps
- AI Safety & Guardrails
- Cost Optimization
